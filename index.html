I am from the North of France. A beautiful region called Pas-De-Calais where we name ourselves “Ch'ti”. The saying goes that a stranger cries twice when he comes here. Once when he arrives (people think it rains all the time) and once when he leaves (saying goodbye to all his new friends)! French is my native language. I learned English in Florida where I spent 3 years. Since then I am fluent in English. I am honest, loyal, passionate and with strong ethics.

I am a programmer

I started when I was 16 years old by toying with html, css and javascript. I studied computer science at Skema and at the Florida Institute of Technology. I have a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science. I enjoy programming in Ruby and Haskell. My fortes are Web development and iOS. I have been working professionally for 10 years and it has been 18 years since my beginnings on the emerging web.

I am an Artist

I love mixing digital and classic techniques. I particularly enjoy typography and drawing. I am proficient with Photoshop and Illustrator. I have an eye for ‘pixel perfect details’ and this gives me an edge in my work where programming and art meet.

I am a self-learner

I enjoy reading and learning about anything. It can be Art, Computer Science, marketing, management, sport…. There are tons of things I want to try out, learn and be better at. I feel there’s never enough time to do everything I want. Though I’m really trying!

Currently

I am mainly working on personal projects: iOS Apps & Web Apps. However I am open to opportunities. I work remotely. I am a paraglider and I often dream of my next flights :).
